Utah County Single Adults sponsor two firesides a month, held on the 2nd and 4th Sunday unless otherwise posted.
Each fireside is followed by an opportunity to mingle and enjoy light refreshments.
Mid-Single Adults age 31-45 are welcome to join us.
As with all UCSA activities, church standards of dress and behavior apply.
