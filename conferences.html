UCSA Winter Conference

This is going to be so good! We invite you to kick off your new year with Sister Reyna Aburto, Anthony Sweat, Matt Townsend, Sharon and Cecil Samuelson, Jared Halverson and more!

Also speaking: Lori Draper, Dr. Paul Jenkins and Jen Zingmark! Saturday night there will be a concert by Charley Jenkins!

January 17-21, 2024

  • Ages 46+ and 31+ are welcome to attend
  • No registration is required to attend the conference!
  • Attending the conference is free, except for meals & dances on Friday and Saturday
  • The total cost for 2 dinners, 1 lunch, and 2 dances is only $25! ($5 each)
  • You are also welcome to bring your own food if you want

Conference Standards

Standards and behaviors of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints are to be followed.

  • Nice, casual dress for all firesides, workshops & dances
  • Sunday dress for the Sunday closing keynote

Conferences are for single adults only. All divorces must be final.
