Conference Standards

Standards and behaviors of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints are to be followed.

  • Nice, casual dress for all firesides, workshops & dances
  • Sunday dress for the Sunday closing keynote

Conferences are for single adults only. All divorces must be final.
