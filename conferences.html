UCSA Conferences

We have historically held conferences twice a year, typically in January and June,
to provide spiritual enrichment and community building opportunities for single adults in Utah County.

Stay posted for more info about future conferences!

Conference Standards

Standards and behaviors of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints are to be followed.

  • Nice, casual dress for all firesides, workshops & dances
  • Sunday dress for the Sunday closing keynote

Conferences are organized for single adults 46 and older. Singles 36 and older are also welcome. All attendees must be legally single.
