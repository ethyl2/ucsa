DANCE STANDARDS

Latter-Day Saints standards and behaviors are to be followed.
Tasteful and modest dress is required:
• No shorts or skirts     • No low-cut, strapless, tank-tops, or revealing clothing     • No tight-fitting or ragged clothing
• No clothing with holes or torn     • No extreme or immodest clothing
Dances are for Single Adults Only. All divorces must be final.
We reserve the right to ask anyone who violates these standards to leave the dance.
