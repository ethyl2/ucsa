All Single Adults age 31+ that love to sing, want to make friends, share their talents, and worship through music are invited to join!
All voice parts (Soprano, Alto, Tenor, Bass) are welcome!
The Utah County Single Adult Choir performs concert firesides each year for Christmas and Easter, plus a Pops Concert of show tunes in August.
All choir concert firesides are free and open to the general public to allow family and friends of the choir to attend.
