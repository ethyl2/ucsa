The Payson Coordinating Council brings a monthly Payson Temple opportunity to the Utah County Single Adults.

Join with other single adults for a heart-warming breakfast at the church located next to the Payson Temple, from 8:15 - 9:20 am.

Breakfast is followed by attending the Payson Temple together as a group to perform proxy sealings. This group has reserved time at 9 am and 10 am to be in the temple.

Teddy Bears Project

Each month at our Lehi dance we have a service project. Lately we've been stuffing teddy bears to be given to children in ambulances, hospitals and refugee camps around the world. Over the last few months, we've stuffed over 100 teddy bears! We have a great time! Come join us!
