Temple Sessions & Activities

UCSA Temple Night

1st Wednesday of each month

  • Temple Session 7 pm
  • Mingle at the church building next to the temple afterward, 8:30-10 pm

Note: PLEASE RESERVE YOUR OWN APPOINTMENTS for the 7 pm endowment session or 7:30 pm sealing session here on the church's website.

Or if you are unable to make a reservation, reach out to the UCSA presidency or just come! They will make extra reservations each month.

And if everything is full, the temple will have a place for you to relax and meditate.

The Payson Coordinating Council brings a monthly Payson Temple opportunity to the Utah County Single Adults.

Join with other single adults for a heart-warming breakfast at the church located next to the Payson Temple, from 8:15 - 9:20 am.

Breakfast is followed by attending the Payson Temple together as a group to perform proxy sealings. This group has reserved time at 9 am and 10 am to be in the temple.
