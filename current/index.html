Utah County Single Adults (UCSA) is organized for Latter-Day Saint single adults age 46 and up.

Any mid-singles age 31-45 that want to participate with us are also welcome.

UCSA is under Priesthood leadership of the Area Authority Seventy & a Coordinating Council of representatives from Latter-day Saint stakes and wards throughout Utah County.

We invite you to come to our events and activities!

All divorces must be final.

No married couples.
