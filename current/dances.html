Dance Standards

Standards and behaviors of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints are to be followed.

Tasteful and modest dress is required:

  • No shorts or mini-skirts
  • No low-cut, strapless, tank-tops, or revealing clothing
  • No tight-fitting or ragged clothing
  • No clothing with holes or torn
  • No extreme or immodest clothing

Dances are for single adults only. All divorces must be final.

No married couples.

We reserve the right to ask anyone who violates these standards to leave the dance.
